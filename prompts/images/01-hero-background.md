# Hero Background — Abstract Email Flow

**Save as:** `assets/images/hero-bg.png`
**Dimensions:** 1920x1080
**Use:** Homepage hero section background, behind white headline text. Must be subtle enough to not compete with overlaid text.

---

Create a high-quality, ultra-sharp, crisp image at maximum resolution. Every edge must be clean and well-defined. No blur, no softness, no compression artifacts. Pixel-perfect rendering.

Generate an image with the following specifications. Follow every detail precisely.

**Subject:** Abstract visualization of email data flowing through security authentication checkpoints. Geometric email envelope shapes dissolve into streams of verified data particles as they pass through translucent hexagonal shield gates. The composition flows from the left side to the right side.

**Exact color palette — use these hex values precisely:**
- Background: pure dark slate #0F172A
- Primary glow/trails: deep teal #0F766E
- Secondary glow: indigo #4338CA
- Accent highlights (very sparse): amber #F59E0B
- Light particles: white #FFFFFF at low opacity

**Composition:**
- Wide format, 1920x1080 pixels (16:9)
- The visual elements are concentrated in the center and lower half — the upper portion should be darker and emptier because headline text will be overlaid there
- Flowing left-to-right movement
- Subtle grid/dot pattern barely visible in the deep background
- Soft glowing particles scattered throughout

**Style rules:**
- Extremely minimal — lots of negative space, only essential elements
- No text, no UI elements, no icons, no logos, no readable characters
- Soft, diffused lighting — no harsh highlights
- Glass morphism feel — translucent shapes with soft edges
- Premium SaaS aesthetic — think Linear.app or Raycast.com hero backgrounds
- The image must work as a BACKGROUND — it should be atmospheric, not the focal point
- No photorealism, no stock photo elements, no people
- Smooth gradients, no banding
- Pure dark #0F172A background, no lighter background bleed behind elements

**What to avoid:**
- Busy or cluttered composition — this goes behind text
- Bright or high-contrast areas in the top half
- Generic gradient blobs (the Tailwind template cliché)
- Recognizable objects — keep it abstract
- Any text or letter-like shapes
- Blurry, soft, or out-of-focus rendering — every edge and line must be razor-sharp and crisp
