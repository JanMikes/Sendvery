# Logo Concept — Paper Plane + Checkmark

**Save as:** `assets/images/logo-concept-3.png`
**Dimensions:** square
**Use:** Logo concept exploration ONLY. The final logo must be recreated as a vector (SVG). This is for direction, not production use.

---

Create a high-quality, ultra-sharp, crisp image at maximum resolution. Every edge must be clean and well-defined. No blur, no softness, no compression artifacts. Pixel-perfect rendering.

Generate an image with the following specifications. Follow every detail precisely.

**Subject:** A minimal logo icon concept that combines a paper plane (representing email sending) with a checkmark (representing verification/health). The checkmark could be formed by the plane's tail, wing angle, or trail. This symbolizes "verified, healthy email delivery." Show 4 variations of this concept in a 2x2 grid.

**Exact colors:**
- Icon: single solid color, deep teal #0F766E
- Background: pure white #FFFFFF

**Composition:**
- 4 distinct variations arranged in a 2x2 grid
- Variation 1: paper plane in flight, its contrail/trail behind it forms a checkmark
- Variation 2: paper plane where the folded wing creates a checkmark angle
- Variation 3: paper plane viewed from the side, the tail section IS a checkmark
- Variation 4: abstract/minimal — just a few geometric lines that simultaneously read as both a paper plane and a checkmark

**Style rules:**
- Flat vector style — no gradients, no 3D, no shadows, no textures
- Single color only (teal on white)
- Ultra-minimal — must be recognizable at 16x16 pixels (favicon size)
- Clean geometric lines — angular, dynamic, suggesting forward movement
- The icon should work as: favicon, navbar logo, app icon
- Each variation distinctly different
- The dual reading (plane AND check) should be clever but not forced — if someone sees only a plane or only a checkmark, that's fine too

**What to avoid:**
- Complex or detailed shapes
- Multiple colors or gradients
- Cute/playful style — keep it sharp and professional
- Realistic paper plane rendering
- Any text or letters
- The checkmark being a separate element — it must be integrated into the plane's form
- Blurry, soft, or out-of-focus rendering — every edge and line must be razor-sharp and crisp
