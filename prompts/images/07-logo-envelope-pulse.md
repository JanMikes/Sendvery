# Logo Concept — Envelope + Pulse

**Save as:** `assets/images/logo-concept-1.png`
**Dimensions:** square
**Use:** Logo concept exploration ONLY. The final logo must be recreated as a vector (SVG) in a design tool. This is for direction, not production use.

---

Create a high-quality, ultra-sharp, crisp image at maximum resolution. Every edge must be clean and well-defined. No blur, no softness, no compression artifacts. Pixel-perfect rendering.

Generate an image with the following specifications. Follow every detail precisely.

**Subject:** A minimal logo icon concept that combines a stylized email envelope with a heartbeat/pulse line running through it. The pulse line enters from the left, creates the top V-shape of the envelope (the flap), then continues out the right — so the envelope shape IS the pulse. This symbolizes "email health monitoring." Show 4 variations of this concept in a 2x2 grid.

**Exact colors:**
- Icon: single solid color, deep teal #0F766E
- Background: pure white #FFFFFF

**Composition:**
- 4 distinct variations arranged in a 2x2 grid
- Each variation explores a slightly different interpretation of the envelope-pulse concept
- Variation 1: the pulse line forms the envelope's top flap
- Variation 2: the pulse line runs horizontally through the middle of the envelope
- Variation 3: the entire envelope outline is drawn in a continuous pulse/heartbeat style
- Variation 4: a simplified approach — envelope with a small pulse symbol inside

**Style rules:**
- Flat vector style — no gradients, no 3D, no shadows, no textures
- Single color only (teal on white)
- Ultra-minimal — must be recognizable at 16x16 pixels (favicon size)
- Clean geometric lines — no hand-drawn feel
- The icon should work as: favicon (16x16), navbar (32px height), app icon (512x512)
- Think of icons like Linear's, Notion's, or Stripe's logomark — simple, geometric, instantly recognizable
- Each variation should be distinctly different, not minor tweaks

**What to avoid:**
- Detailed or complex shapes — simplicity is paramount
- Multiple colors
- Rounded/bubbly/playful style — keep it sharp and professional
- Realistic envelope rendering
- Any text or letters in the icon itself
- Thick line weights — keep lines medium-thin and consistent
- Blurry, soft, or out-of-focus rendering — every edge and line must be razor-sharp and crisp
